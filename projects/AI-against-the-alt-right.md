# AI against the alt right.

A new type of right thinking has recently emerged in the internet, known as _alt-right_. Its stance against liberalism is not this time in terms of what is the right thing to do in terms of morality, but it emphasizes more on a discussion about what is really possible and distorted views about what really __is__. Its most identifying phrase is that of Ben Shapiro: "Reality doesn't care about your feelings".

As a discussed idea between me and Ilan Manouach, we decided to start the project of the [AI-against the alt right](https://twitter.com/radicaldumb) by collecting a big amount of alt-right twitter users and crawling their tweets.
We trained a language model on this type of language and we managed to successfully create a mapping object of the Alt-Right, which made to be inspired and respond to trending tweets.
On our newest iteration we try to engage the alt-right generator into replying to posts of other alt-right people, by crawling big amounts of twitter posts, to make this mapping even more interactive.

If you like this project and you want to support it, consider following it on Twitter.
