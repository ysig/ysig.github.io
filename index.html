I'm a postdoctoral researcher in the Pioneer Center for AI in Copenhagen, working on modeling Digital Narratives, under the supervision of Serge Belongie. I defended my PhD in Computer Vision on "Image Mining via Synthesis" (2022-2025) at Ecole Des Ponts, advised by Mathieu Aubry, where I collaborated closely with Alyosha Efros and Shiry Ginosar. I'm a graduate of MVA and ECE-NTUA.

Being a polymath the core of my research is interdisciplinary, formed around three central beliefs: a) deep neural networks are the first technology to operationalize human perception outside reductive mathematical models; b) being statistical sciences of perception, the "arts" and the "humanities" are ideal generators of machine learning problems; c) by operationalizing perceptual ontological questions, machine-learning turns modeling into philosophy-in-action.
